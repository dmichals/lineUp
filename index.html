What it does
Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.
01
GPS Start Line Geometry
Mark the PIN and RC boat ends independently — in either order. Real-time distance to each end, perpendicular distance to the line, and favored end calculation — all from your device's built-in GPS.
02
Burn Time Calculator
Always-visible burn time based on your current speed and distance to the line. Counts down to zero at the gun. Flips to GO when it's time to head for the line at full speed.
03
True Wind Direction
Point the bow into the wind and tap Head to Wind — LineUp records the true wind direction instantly. Or enter it manually. Used for accurate favored end calculation and line bias in degrees.
04
Speed to Line
Real-time target speed showing exactly how fast to sail to reach the line at the gun. Color-coded delta tells you to ease off or trim on. Always visible alongside your current SOG.
05
Precise Beep Schedule
Audible signals at every minute mark, the 30-second mark, every second inside 10 seconds, and a distinct higher tone for the final 5 seconds. Never miss a gun signal.
06
Works on Anything
Runs in any modern browser. Android, iPad, iPhone, laptop. No App Store. No install. No account. Add to your home screen for a full-screen experience. Daylight-optimised light theme.
Race day
Up and running
in four steps.
1
Open LineUp in Chrome or Safari
Allow location when prompted. GPS locks in 30–60 seconds on open water. No install needed.
2
Set wind & start line
Head into the wind, tap Head to Wind. Then sail to PIN end, tap Mark PIN. Sail to RC boat, tap Mark RC.
3
Sync at the gun
Tap SYNC the moment the 5-minute warning gun fires. Use RESYNC at any subsequent gun to stay locked in.
4
Hit the line perfectly
Watch burn time count to GO, match your speed to the Target, and aim for the favored end.
